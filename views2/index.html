About Me

I have been formally involved in software development since I was 15, although I was fascinated by technology long before. It was not uncommon to see spare bits of motors or wires ripped apart and electronic components strewn around my room (much to my mother's ire) but this fascination with technology has grown O(n2) with age. Its pinnacle set in with my formal study of Computer Science. I am also fascinated by Mathematical Logic and it's foundations (I'm looking at you Godel), analysis of language, as spurred by Chomsky and Cognitive Science with a focus on Carl Jung.

At my core, I have a natural and insatiable curiosity that leads me to pick apart everything in my vicinity to discover how it ticks, and this has strongly helped my output as a developer. I am very confident in my skills and knowledge that I have accumulated thus far, but this is only the beginning, and I am excited for what is to come. At this point, I am looking for opportunities in the Cryptocurrency/Blockchain space. It offers the most exciting and feasible opportunity in the history of humanity to have a fully decentralized society and I want to be at the forefront of this revolution. Feel free to email me or reach out to me on social media to geek out about any of these topics!

Languages I have used: Javascript (Node.js), Solidity, C, Java, Python, Bash, Assembly (x86), C++, MATLAB, HTML/CSS, XML, MySQL
